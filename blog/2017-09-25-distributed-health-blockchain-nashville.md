In September 2017 I attended [Distributed: Health](https://health.distributed.com/) in
Nashville, the flagship conference on blockchain in healthcare. These are my notes.

## The event

- **When:** September 25–26, 2017 (the second annual edition)
- **Where:** Schermerhorn Symphony Center, downtown Nashville
- **Host:** BTC Inc / BTC Media — billed as the first and largest conference for
  blockchain advancement in healthcare
- **Featured speakers:** healthcare leaders from Aetna, the CDC, and the Altarum
  Institute, including:
  - **Dr. Harold Paz** — Executive VP & Chief Medical Officer, Aetna
  - **Jim Nasr** — Chief Software Architect, CDC (Center for Surveillance,
    Epidemiology and Laboratory Services)
  - **Francois de Brantes** — VP & Director, Altarum Institute Center for Payment
    Innovation
- **Kickoff panel:** "A New Infrastructure for Healthcare," with **John Bass**
  (CEO, Hashed Health) and **Joe Murad** (CEO, PokitDok).
