<https://github.com/dg1an3/pheonixrt>

Three projects are represented:

  * [pheonixrt](https://github.com/dg1an3/pheonixrt) is the original inverse planning algorithm based on the convolutional input layer
  * [WarpTPS](https://github.com/dg1an3/WarpTPS) is the interactive morphing using TPSs
  * [ALGT](https://github.com/dg1an3/ALGT) is the predicate verification tools

Also, see the references at the end of <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thin_plate_spline> for some videos showing WarpTPS.
