Image review is critical to modern [IGRT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image-guided_radiation_therapy), and will be equally important for adaptive RT.  Yet today most users complain that it is a painful chore.  So how do we make image review better?

I think three areas are critical:

  1. A [better image review user experience](http://www.dg1an3.net/2017/03/better-image-review-experience.html)
  2. Better workflows to support image review
  3. Advanced analysis tools to assist in the review process

I'll post my thoughts on areas of improvement for each of these.
